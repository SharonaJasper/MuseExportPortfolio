Small Design Cases

Lawn Mower

 

This assignment was all about designing a small lawn mower for urban users with small gardens. The focus of this lawn mower should be the user experience in small spaces, the connection to the selected brand, Gardena, and the desired appearance in accordance with a personal style collage. No accurate elaboration on the mechanical operation is needed, but the exterior design should at least provide enough feasibility for it.

Payment Method for Cryptocurrencies

 

In three submissions, the possibilities for a cryptocurrency payment method was investigated, based on the meaning and values people attach to products and style. With this focus, two existing models were used. For the second part of the assignment the Product Experience Model was used. The third and final part of the assignment was based on the Path to Long-Term Usage Model. This entire assignment had the restriction of not using more than 200 words per submission, resulting in more visual explanations.
