Light Cycle Lamps

The Light Cycle Lamps is a smart system concept designed for Bruggerbosch, that helps people with dementia finding the bathroom at night. It also shows them the way back to their room and makes them feel safe at same time. It helps them finding their way without waking up other residents or panicking. The product empowers the dementia patient by increasing the self-reliance.

 

During some consults with Bruggerbosch, we acquired a lot of knowledge about these specific users and specifically how their attention can be drawn. It turned out that everything needed to be as intuitive as possible, avoiding written directions and using greater incentives than other diversions. At the end of the design process we did the testing with two residents, from which the effectiveness of this application could not be determined accurately, but was not considered scary or dissuasive. If we were to have more time, the testing could have been extended with several greater incentives than the LED strip and automatic lights.
