Natural Cosmetic Lines

Natural Cosmetic Lines is a permanent make up business in Twente, which wanted to make a fresh start after having been out of the game for a while. Their old house style was completely changed to a sharper, calmer and fresher design, with grey scale colours and yellow accents.

This make-over included the logo, website, business cards, flyers and document layouts.
