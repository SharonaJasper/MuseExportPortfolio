Dies & Almanac

Audentis

The Dies Commission of student association Audentis et Virtutis is responsible for the planning and execution of Audentis' Dies Natalis week in a full-time period of approximately fifteen weeks. This means providing a memorable week for all members in honour of the association's birthday from scratch. In addition to these private events, some events involve other students as well, giving them a glimpse of the most glorious week in Enschede. In march 2018, the 37th Dies Natalis took place, themed Paradies (Paradise). Within this 37th Dies commission my function was the design of the logo, printed matter, making advertisements for sponsors, designs for painted event signs, etc.

 

On the other hand, the Audentis Almanac Commission takes care of the assembly, layout and contents of a complete bookwork about this association, called an Almanac. This book is handed out during the Dies Natalis week. The 2018 Almanac, themed Imaginair (Imaginary), was a part-time group effort of 4 people.
